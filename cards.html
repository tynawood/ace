Debit Cards

  • Access Card

The Accesscard is an internationally accepted Visa Debit Card which can be used online or across over 1 million Visa ATMs and PoS worldwide.

 

  • Accesslink Card (Classic)

The accesslink card is a proprietory card that can be used on any Gh-link enabled ATM or Point of Sale terminal in Ghana.

 

  • Accesslink card (Gold)

Accesslink Gold a local card for High Network Individuals (HNIs) that allows them to conduct banking transactions via a GH-link enabled ATM or Point of Sale terminal across the country.

 

Card holders enjoy exclusive discounts at partner leisure and hospitality centres.

 

  • Prepaid Card

The Access Bank Visa Prepaid card is an internationally accepted debit card which can be acquired with or without an operational account with Access Bank. It gives you access to load cedis and spend anywhere in the world.

 

Credit Cards

  • Visa Credit Card

The Visa Credit card is an internationally accepted Visa Credit Card which can be used for online transactions and across over 1 million Visa ATMs and PoS worldwide.
