Mobile Banking

Access Mobile

Access Mobile is an electronic payment solution that enables account holders securely and conveniently carry out transactions such as funds transfer and payment for goods and services using their mobile phones or devices anywhere and anytime.

 

Characteristics

Purpose:

  • Provide access to accounts in the comfort of their location 24/7
  • Provide convenience
  • Saves time and cost

 

Features

  • Funds Transfer
  • Airtime purchase
  • Mini Statement of last 5 transactions
  • Balance Enquiry
  • DSTV and GOTV payments
